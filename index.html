About Me

Hoang Duy
Phong Tran

Interior Designer · Project Manager

With over a decade of international project experience spanning Vietnam, Australia, and East Asia, I bring a cross-cultural design sensibility to every space. My work balances Eastern refinement with Western functionality — creating interiors that are authentic, purposeful, and long-lasting.

Currently completing my AAS in Interior Design at Des Moines Area Community College (Class of 2026), where I have engaged in hotel, commercial, residential, and lighting design projects under the guidance of experienced faculty. I am proficient in Revit, SketchUp, Enscape, D5 Render, AutoCAD, and the Adobe Suite, and I have built custom AI web tools to accelerate design workflows.

My travels to Japan, Taiwan, Australia, Singapore, and China have deeply shaped how I approach design — every project is an opportunity to blend cultural perspectives into a space that feels both universal and specific.
