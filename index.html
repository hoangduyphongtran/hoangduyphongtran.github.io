About Me

Hoang Duy
Phong Tran

Interior Designer · Project Manager

With over a decade of international project experience spanning Vietnam, Australia, and East Asia, I bring a cross-cultural design sensibility to every space. My work balances Eastern refinement with Western functionality — creating interiors that are authentic, purposeful, and long-lasting.

Currently completing my AAS in Interior Design at Des Moines Area Community College (Class of 2026), proficient in Revit, SketchUp, Enscape, D5 Render, AutoCAD, and the Adobe Suite. I have also built custom AI web tools to accelerate design workflows.

My travels to Japan, Taiwan, Australia, Singapore, and China have deeply shaped my approach — blending cultural perspectives into spaces that feel both universal and deeply personal.
